About Us

Central Florida Tapping and Construction Services is a licensed underground contractor with a Class 5 Fire License. Located in Longwood, CFT is Central Florida’s premier Tapping and Line Stop Contractor. CFT installs fire and water mains. CFT performance history has provided us the opportunity to work with many government agencies, Cities, Counties, Municipalities, General Contractors and the best Site Development Contractors in Central Florida. CFT maintains a fleet of company owned equipment, trucks and trailers. CFT staff are always the most professional and each team member values our partnerships that have been developed for over 30 years. CFT crews are willing to problem solve with our clients for the betterment of every project.

About Us

CFT has a 24 hour emergency response team on call and is currently contracted with several local Cities, Counties, Municipalities. CFT is on call for any water mains or services, force main and lift station leaks, breakages and total washouts. CFT is a member of NUCA, National Utility Contractors Association. Our Owner and President as well as our Operations Manager have both been members of NUCA for over 20 years each. Rick Bullington sits on the Board of Directors and currently serves as Treasurer while Vinson Gammage is on the Board of Directors and is Vice President of the Central and East Coast Division. With the collected experience of our management team and employees, we are certain to be a valued partner in every aspect and phase of your construction projects.
