About Us

Established in 1996, Central Florida Tapping & Construction Services, Inc., originally focused on Wet Taps and Line Stops. However, with a background in a variety of Underground Projects we have grown into a company of 5 pipe crews and 2 tapping trucks that are constantly on the move. We have built a solid reputation of dependability, cooperation, with a solid Team mentality. We maintain a Building Contractor, Underground Utility Contractor, and Class V Contractors License. If you think we might could be of service to you, please contact us.

Each year we gather to celebrate our partners & our legacy. CFT has served many people around central florida, and will continue to do so for years to come. Here we have the Team that makes everything possible. Keep an eye out for our trucks on the next job site you visit!

Contact us today and share your project with us. We are open to a wide range of projects. Through the years, we have developed multiple lasting partnerships due to our honesty and integrity.
