About Us

Established in 1996, central Florida tapping originally focused on tapping and misc. repair work. Since then, CFT has grown into one of the most respected contractors of central Florida. With 4 fully loaded fleets and a professional crew to accommodate, CFT today is ready to offer solutions ranging from taps to underground fire installation. Our core principles are honesty, quality, and efficiency. These core values have allowed us to build partnerships through the years and is how we have earned our reputation.

Each year we gather to celebrate our partners & our legacy. CFT has served many people around central florida, and will continue to do so for years to come. Here we have the Team that makes everything possible. Keep an eye out for our trucks on the next job site you visit!

Central Florida Tapping and Construction Services is a licensed underground contractor with a Class 5 Fire License. Located in Longwood, CFT is Central Florida’s premier Tapping and Line Stop Contractor. CFT installs fire and water mains. CFT performance history has provided us the opportunity to work with many government agencies, Cities, Counties, Municipalities, General Contractors and the best Site Development Contractors in Central Florida. CFT maintains a fleet of company owned equipment, trucks and trailers. CFT staff are always the most professional and each team member values our partnerships that have been developed for over 30 years. CFT crews are willing to problem solve with our clients for the betterment of every project.
